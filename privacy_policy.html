Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

Welcome to Meeplr! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our tabletop gaming matchmaking application. By using Meeplr, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Meeplr is operated by Caitlyn Carmabella Nayeli ("we," "us," or "our"). Meeplr is a mobile application designed to help adult tabletop gamers (18+) find compatible gaming partners in their area.

2. Age Requirement

Meeplr is strictly an adult-only platform (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we discover that we have collected information from someone under 18, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a user is under 18, please report them to us at carma@meeplrapp.com.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, display name, email address, date of birth, pronouns, and bio
  • Profile Information: Custom meeple avatar design, profile photos (optional), gaming preferences
  • Location Information: City, state, and country (we do NOT collect precise GPS coordinates)
  • Gaming Preferences: Favorite games, skill levels, play styles, online platforms, and gaming availability schedule
  • Communication: Messages sent through our chat system, group messages, and session planning details

3.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent on the app, interaction patterns
  • Push Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for Android or Apple Push Notification Service (APNS) tokens for iOS to send you notifications
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Core Services

  • Matchmaking: We use your location (city-level), game preferences, availability, and play styles to suggest compatible gaming partners
  • Profile Display: Your profile information is shown to other users when you appear in their matches
  • Communication: Enable messaging, group chats, and session planning between matched users
  • Notifications: Send push notifications about new matches, messages, session invitations, and app updates

4.2 Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Process reports of inappropriate behavior and enforce our community standards
  • Maintain blocked user lists and enforce user blocks
  • Preserve evidence in cases of harassment or safety concerns

4.3 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve matchmaking algorithms
  • Understand how users interact with features to enhance user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior

5. Information Sharing

5.1 With Other Users

We share your profile information (display name, username, bio, meeple avatar, gaming preferences, and location) with other users when:

  • You appear in their match suggestions
  • You connect with them
  • You join a gaming session or group together
  • You send them messages

5.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who help us operate Meeplr:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications for Android
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Push notifications for iOS
  • BoardGameGeek API: Game information and metadata (no personal data shared)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for providing services to Meeplr.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Legal process (subpoena, court order, etc.)
  • Law enforcement requests
  • Protection of our rights or safety of our users
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues

5.4 Business Transfers

If Meeplr is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or app notification before your information is transferred.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Active Accounts

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide you services.

6.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the Account Settings page. When you delete your account:

  • Immediately Deleted: Your profile information, gaming preferences, availability, location data, connections, swipes, blocked users, and reports
  • Anonymized (Not Deleted): Your chat messages remain visible but are anonymized. The sender name is removed and replaced with "[Deleted User]". This preserves conversation history for other users and maintains evidence for safety investigations.
  • Why Keep Messages? We retain messages in anonymized form to:
    • Maintain conversation context for other users
    • Preserve evidence for harassment investigations
    • Comply with legal data retention requirements

6.3 Inactive Accounts

Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted. We will notify you via email before deletion.

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Correction

You can access and update your profile information at any time through the app's Edit Profile section.

7.2 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data by emailing carma@meeplrapp.com. We will provide your data in a portable format within 30 days.

7.3 Deletion

You can delete your account and most associated data through the app. See Section 6.2 above for details on what is deleted vs. anonymized.

7.4 Marketing Opt-Out

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email or by adjusting your notification settings in the app.

7.5 Push Notifications

You can control push notifications through your device settings or in-app notification preferences.

8. Security Measures

We take data security seriously and implement various measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS
  • Authentication: Secure authentication through Supabase Auth
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to user data
  • Monitoring: Regular security monitoring and updates
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Meeplr is NOT for children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age. If you are under 18, do not use Meeplr or provide any information to us. Parents or guardians who discover their child has provided information to Meeplr should contact us immediately at carma@meeplrapp.com and we will delete that information promptly.

10. International Users

Meeplr is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using Meeplr, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to delete personal information (with exceptions for legal obligations)
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do NOT sell your information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at carma@meeplrapp.com.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal/regulatory reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For material changes, we will notify you via email or in-app notification
  • Your continued use of Meeplr after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. Third-Party Links

Meeplr may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as BoardGameGeek for game information). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

14. Your Consent

By using Meeplr, you consent to:

  • The collection and use of your information as described in this policy
  • The sharing of your profile information with other Meeplr users
  • The transfer of your information to the United States
  • The retention of anonymized messages after account deletion
