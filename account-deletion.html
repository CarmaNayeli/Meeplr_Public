Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Method 1: Delete In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the Meeplr app:

  1. Open the Meeplr app
  2. Go to your Profile tab
  3. Scroll to the bottom
  4. Tap "Delete My Account"
  5. Follow the confirmation steps

Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email from your registered email address
  2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your username or email used for the account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, bio, location, photos)
  • All your game preferences and availability
  • All your connections and matches
  • All your messages and chat history
  • All reports you've filed
  • Your blocked users list
  • Your authentication credentials
