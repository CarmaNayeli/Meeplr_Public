Meeplr is strictly for adults aged 18 and over. We take the protection of minors seriously and have implemented multiple safeguards to prevent underage access to our platform.

Our Commitment

At Meeplr, we are committed to maintaining a safe, adult-only environment for tabletop gaming enthusiasts to connect. We recognize our responsibility to prevent minors from accessing our service and have established comprehensive standards to protect children.

Meeplr is operated by Caitlyn Carmabella Nayeli, and we take child safety as our highest priority. No minor should ever access or use our platform.

Age Verification

Multiple Layers of Protection:

  • Registration Check: All users must certify they are 18 years of age or older during account registration
  • Date of Birth: Users provide their date of birth during the signup process
  • Automatic Rejection: Accounts that indicate underage status are immediately rejected and cannot be created
  • Additional Verification: We reserve the right to request additional verification if we suspect false information has been provided

What Verification May Include

If we suspect a user may be underage, we may request:

  • Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, national ID)
  • Credit card verification for premium subscribers
  • Video verification call

Timeline: Users must provide verification within 7 days of request. Failure to comply results in immediate account suspension.

Monitoring and Enforcement

Active Monitoring

  • Our moderation team actively monitors for signs of underage users
  • Automated systems flag suspicious profiles and behavior patterns
  • User reports are reviewed within 24 hours
  • Premium users receive priority in safety reviews

Zero-Tolerance Policy

Immediate Action:

  • Accounts found to belong to minors are immediately suspended
  • Suspected underage accounts are permanently banned upon confirmation
  • IP addresses and device IDs are blocked to prevent re-registration
  • We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for adults who attempt to facilitate minor access to the platform

Consequences for Adults

Adults who knowingly interact with or facilitate underage users face:

  • Immediate permanent account termination
  • Reporting to appropriate authorities
  • Legal action if applicable
  • No refunds for premium subscriptions

Community Responsibility

We encourage our community to help maintain our age standards:

How You Can Help

  • Report suspicious accounts: If you suspect a user may be underage, please report them immediately
  • Do not engage: If you encounter someone who appears to be a minor, do not continue communication and report the account
  • Verify in person: When meeting gaming partners from Meeplr, use common sense and public meeting locations
  • Trust your instincts: If something feels wrong, report it

Privacy and Data Protection

For users who are 18+:

  • We collect only necessary information for matchmaking and communication purposes
  • User data is protected in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations
  • Users have control over their profile visibility and can block or report other users
  • We do not share personal information with third parties without explicit consent
  • Users can request account deletion at any time through Account Settings

See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Content Standards

While Meeplr is an adult platform, we maintain community standards:

  • Content must be appropriate for a public gaming community
  • Harassment, explicit content, and inappropriate behavior are prohibited
  • Our moderation team reviews reported content and takes action against violations
  • Users who violate community standards face account suspension or termination

See our Terms of Service for complete conduct rules.
